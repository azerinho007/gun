ConvNets

Convolutional neural networks or also called convnets are one of the most exciting developments in machine learning in recent years.
This approche is composed by a principal sequence of measurements.

1.Convolution layer

It is processed even for 1,2 or 3 Dimension type of data. In this phase we want to extract some features from the data, like human vision cortex.
2.Pooling layer

As a further process, we want to reduce the dimension of params, thus, after a convolution laying we extract much more deep features based on type of pooling method.
3.Flatten layer

As a last layer after extracting the features in a reduced dimension, we need to flatten it as a one big scalar vector to feed it to a deep neural network to perform our classification.
4.Standard architecture of deep neural network
